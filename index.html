1. Introduction & Executive Overview

Level 3 Guardian has a functioning HTTPS website, visible business information, a named CEO, multiple training services, appointment and payment pathways, and a credible foundation for future growth.

The website is not yet structured or edited to the standard needed for strong local rankings or reliable citation by AI answer engines. The most damaging issues are a navigation item labeled “Blank,” repeated headings and text, a generic homepage H1, thin individual service content, grammatical problems, placeholder sections and an FAQ that displays “Loading articles...” instead of crawlable answers.

SEO, AEO and GEO Findings

Traditional SEO

Focus on crawlability, indexation, descriptive page titles, service-page depth, internal linking, schema and local landing pages.

AGEO

Improve direct-answer content, named expert authorship, source citations, entity consistency, original data and citation-ready guides.

Prioritized Recommendations

  1. Proofread the entire website and correct grammar, spelling and formatting.
  2. Replace generic URLs and preserve equity with 301 redirects.
  3. Rewrite the About page around a named instructor and verifiable credentials.
  4. Correct any wording that implies course completion guarantees permit approval.
  5. Separate cancellation policies from individual course descriptions.
  6. Create optimized concealed carry, fundamentals and private-training pages.
  7. Build substantive Raleigh, Durham and RTP location pages.
  8. Add Organization, LocalBusiness, Person, Course, Event, Article and Breadcrumb schema.
  9. Expand the FAQ page and cite official North Carolina sources.
  10. Track calls, bookings, forms, Core Web Vitals, local rankings and AI mentions.
